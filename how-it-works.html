The Boutique Photographer Process

HERE'S HOW IT WORKS

LET'S CHAT

It starts with a quick chat on the phone.

Once you know we are the right fit, we will work together to make sure the experience and artwork we create is custom to you.

We want to get to know all about your family so we can create an experience catered to them that results in artwork that will blend seamlessly with your home.

YOUR SESSION

You session is designed just for you and to be FUN! You will play, laugh, snuggle and be yourselves in front of our camera.

Worried you won't know what to do?

We have your back and will make sure to photograph you and your family authentically.

With over 20 years of experience photographing people, we make sure everyone looks and feels great.

ORDER APPOINTMENT

The moment you've been waiting for!

Sit back and relax as we project your images larger than life. We will hold your hand throughout this process, from choosing your favorite images to selecting wall art to perfectly wall art to perfectly made to compliment your home.

We want you to leave with wall portraits to give your home a look you'll love for generations.
