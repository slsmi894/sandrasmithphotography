The Sandra Smith Boutique Process

Planning with the End in Mind

You need and want family portraits, but what are you going to do with them? At Sandra Smith Photography, we begin with the end in mind, which means, as a boutique photographer, we start with the planning for where and how these beautiful images will hang in your home or the story we want to tell in your family album.

No matter the wall, wide or tall, let's create an amazing iconic image to hang in its place.

No wall space? Telling your family's store in a luxurious, custom-designed album lets you capture and preserve the stage of your children's lives. Your unique needs and concerns will guide your one-on-one Design Consultion.
