I have had many beloved and treasured pets throughout my life. And yes, I do have beautiful images of each. This boxer was a rescue named Buster. I am not sure what his previous life was but he came to the right home where he was spoiled and loved our summer parties! You could see the excitement when he saw that we were setting up tables and such! It took him over six months to open his heart to me, to gain his full trust, and after that he would not let me out of his sight. We had were blessed with two years with him. I love my fur-babies, but he stole a piece of my heart. I truly miss him so much! I am thrilled that I have this beautiful image of him, I was testing out a favorite camera of mine and he obliged me, thankfully. I am so thankful to have this treasured keepsake.

And gently, I will say, I try to get as many images of my loved ones, not just my fur-babies, as I do not have enough, as you never know how fast time flies or what tomorrow holds.
